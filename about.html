Our Story

National Delhi Public School was founded in 2013 with the mission to foster a challenging and supportive environment that encourages critical thinking and holistic development, preparing students for a global society. We believe in nurturing leaders of tomorrow.

Our Core Values

Our Mission

To foster a challenging learning environment that promotes creativity, critical thinking, and the highest standards of academic excellence.

Our Vision

To shape global citizens who are morally upright, intellectually curious, socially responsible, and technologically competent.

Our Promise

To provide personalized guidance and a safe, inclusive atmosphere where every child can achieve their maximum potential.
