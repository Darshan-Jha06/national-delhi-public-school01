Our Simple Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Form

Fill the online inquiry form or collect the registration form from the school office.

2

Assessment

Student interaction/assessment is scheduled for the applicant (Classes I onwards).

3

Confirmation

If selected, submit necessary documents and pay the admission fee to secure the seat.

4

Welcome

Orientation session and student induction before the start of the new academic session.

Key Admission Details

Documents Required for Admission

  • Recent passport-size photographs of the student and parents.
  • Copy of the student's Birth Certificate.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school (Class I onwards).
  • Copy of the last Academic Report Card.
  • Copy of Aadhar Card (Student and Parents).

Age Criteria (As on 31st March 2026)

Nursery: 3+ years | KG: 4+ years | Class I: 5+ years, and so on.

Admission is subject to clearing the entrance assessment and seat availability.

Fee Payment Information

Detailed fee structure is available on the Fee Structure Page.

  • Fees are payable Quarterly (4 Installments).
  • A non-refundable registration fee is applicable upon submission of the form.
